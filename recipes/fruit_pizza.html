Fruit Pizza

This is a paragragh about fruit pizza

Ingredients

  • Fruit
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Cookie Dough
  • Vanilla Extract

Directions

  1. Mix butter, sugar, cream cheese, and extract
  2. Make cookie into crust
  3. Bake cookie til golden brown
  4. Take out, let cool then spread cream cheese mixture on it
  5. Place in fridge to chill
